UPI Payments

TimePay UPI – Instant Mobile Transfer


NPST envisioned the growth of digital platform which would be empowered by UPI payment ecosystem. This led to our decision in becoming one of the Third Party UPI Application (TPAP) in the country enabling merchants and users to harness the advantage built through integrated ecosystem of UPI.


Being a Technology Service Provider (TSP) to banks we have developed robust solution for UPI as per the NPCI’s guidelines.


Our step towards TPAP was to create an integrated ecosystem empowering several Merchant categories in MSME segment who are deprived of the digital ERP inbuilt with Payment platform.


This new payment method can now allow user transaction from any application linking any bank account. A complete interoperable platform with QR based payment features for Merchants to accept digital payments.


TimePay®, developed by NPST and sponsored by The Cosmos Co-op Bank ltd is a solution which synthesizes Digital payments with consumer services and merchant platform. It is most simplified and efficient application to manage user Accounts and Merchant Payments with Security.


TimePay® empowers Merchant with an automated process, integrated payment, data intelligence, and digital services. It is the ‘one-stop solution for your multiple needs with quick and easy process.
